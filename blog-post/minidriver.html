Mini Driver

Posted on January 4, 2023 by MissKonstruction

I will always consider them my babies...however they are anything but.

I finally got used to my son driving and being in college and starting his adult life...

and now my daughter is driving too.

She is such a good driver...

only thing she's done so far is accidentally back over the mailbox. We spent the last year with her driving to school every morning while I rode with her. It was enjoyable and i will miss it, but i also proud that she has been taking herself and her friend Ashlyn to school and back safely and responsibly.

My feelings are so mixed...on one hand i want them to stay young and at home forever. The other is excited for their journies through life to start.

This isn't a real blog but the posts definitely are real.
Your first clue that this isn't a real blog should be the comments section i deliberately coded and that you can't add to. Yes they are my favorite quotes from the Conjuring. Fight me.

But seriously...

It was hard for me when i was a teenager. i was never really trusted with Important Things. It left a scar. To this day i'm unsure of my true worth and value to others.
So I have made it my life's goal to always communicate to my kids that i take them seriously, they matter on a level they will never comprehend,
and i will do anything for them.
Speaking of anything...this website is a hobby for me. Though I am a developer for hire depending on what you want, I have a great job right now. I do not rely on ads to keep this site going. I coded the entire thing myself with bootstrap framework and host it for free on Github. That being said, I have added a donation to St Jude link. If you're here please donate. It would mean a lot. Thank you.

Hurricane Ian

Posted on September 26, 2022 by MissKonstruction

Got to leave work early due to being in the red zone.

We haven't done a whole lot to prep; these things usually blow right over...

i know, i know....with that attitude

it will wipe us out for sure.

At any rate i brought the plants in and Marcelo brought my scooter in so we may run out of toilet paper and have to use our hands to wipe our asses.....but hey, the scooter stays dry. It is my only method of transportation after all. Until I get that brand new Honda Accord my 15 year old daughter already talked me out of before i even own it....and we do have water and water bottle filters due to the price gouging Florida vendors are so well-known for during these times...and as long as we don't lose the electricity.....we have a deep freezer that the guy on tv who coaches people who are turning into their parents would have a field day with.

My Bad Ass Spaghetti and Meatballs!

This awesome dish takes minimal prep time and is a guaranteed winner in this house. That being said, we are not a "chunky" sauce loving family. I keep the additives to herbs and spices.

All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti pasta
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/3 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped basil
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped oregano
  • fresh chopped chives
  • 1 egg
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp onion pepper
  • kosher salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. In lg bowl combine beef, bread crumbs, herbs, garlic, egg, and 1 tsp salt
  2. create meatballs with mixture, approximately 1" in diameter
  3. Heat olive oil in large pot over medium-low heat
  4. Add meatballs and cook until all sides are browned
  5. add tomato sauce and bayleaf
  6. simmer 8-10 minutes
  7. serve over spaghetti pasta with garlic bread

Enjoy!

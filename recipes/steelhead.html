My Bad Ass Steelhead Trout!

This awesome dish takes less time prepping than most of the sides of your choice will.
We went with steamed brussel sprouts and pasta.

All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 large steelhead trout
  • 1 lemon
  • fresh chopped chives
  • 1 fresh minced garlic clove
  • olive oil
  • Fish Monger seasoning

Instructions

  1. If you're making vegetables and sides start them now
  2. Lightly baste fish with olive oil and lemon juice
  3. Add Chives and spices
  4. cut lemon and place on fish
  5. Image shows using a table-top indoor grill
  6. Oven or outdoor grill perfectly acceptable
  7. Adjust cook times according to method.
    Cook with skin on for best results.
    Though we generally grill outside, for this particular occasion indoors at 300 degrees f for 13 minutes was perfect for us.

Enjoy!

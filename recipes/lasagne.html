My Bad Ass Lasagne!

My family loves this dish. It doesn't take long to make and will feed a family of four for like, three days. Prep time is minimal depending on experience and cook time is a total of about one hour and ten minutes.

All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 lg jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • lasagne noodles
  • 32 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 lb shredded mozzarella cheeses
  • 3/4 lb shredded Parmesan cheese
  • fresh (or dried) parsley or basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 365 degrees F
  2. Cook beef in skillet and drain
  3. Remove from heat, stir in veg broth and marinara
  4. Spread Ricotta evenly over pasta
  5. Spread half Parmesan half mozzarella over the Ricotta
  6. Spread another even layer of meat sauce over cheese layer
  7. Add more lasagne noodles to cover
  8. Spread Ricotta evenly over pasta
  9. Top evenly with half Parmesan half mozzarella
  10. Spread another 1/3 of meat sauce on top of the cheese layer
  11. Add more lasagne noodles and evenly spread the rest of the ricotta on top.
  12. Pour and spread the rest of the meat sauce
  13. Top with remaining shredded cheeses
  14. Cover tightly with foil and bake one hour ten minutes
  15. Uncover and bake ten more minutes
  16. Let rest five minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil.

Enjoy!

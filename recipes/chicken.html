My Bad Ass Baked Chicken!

This is a classic dish made easy and healthy!

All Recipes

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • purple onion
  • olive oil
  • ground sea salt
  • smoked paprika
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. Brine chicken in warm salt water 15 min
  2. Liberally brush both sides of chicken with olive oil
  3. Season both sides with mixture of all ingredients above
  4. Add large chopped pieces of purple onion
  5. Roast chicken uncovered at 450 degrees f for approximately 15-18 minutes depending on breast size/thickness
  6. Allow chicken to rest under lightly covered foil for 5-10 minutes before serving

    7. Enjoy!
